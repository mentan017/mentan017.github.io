About Me

I am Adrian Bernabeu, the son of a Spanish father and a Estonian mother. I was born and raised in Brussels, the capital of Brussels. Since my childhood, I have been exposed to many languages (French, Spanish, Estonian and English), both at home and at school.
In primary, I learned about math and I fell in love with it. I have always done my best to progress in this subject which helped me to become the best in my class, and it has been this way since.

Around that I time discovered programmation, I loved the idea of being able to build my own software from scratch and then use it.

I also consider myself an ambitious person, and for me the ideas of freelancing and creating my own business seem very enticing.
